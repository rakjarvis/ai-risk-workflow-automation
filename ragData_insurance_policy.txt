Insurance Knowledge Base

Claim:
A claim is a request made by a client for compensation due to damage, loss, or incident.
Examples include car accidents, property damage, or personal injury.

Risk:
A risk refers to a potential future event that may cause damage or financial loss.
Examples include cyber attacks, system vulnerabilities, or safety risks.

General Inquiry:
General questions about policies, coverage, or documentation.

Risk Types:

Cyber:
Incidents related to data breaches, hacking, ransomware, or IT security.

Liability:
Legal responsibility for damage caused to another person or company.

Property:
Damage to physical assets such as buildings, equipment, or vehicles.

Priority Rules:

High Priority:
- Data breach
- Fire or major damage
- Legal risk
- Business disruption

Medium Priority:
- Partial damage
- Non-critical system issues

Low Priority:
- General questions
- Information requests
